Personality Pairing
in Human-AI Teams

How human and AI personalities combine to shape collaborative outcomes in the lab and in the real world

Harang Ju · Sinan Aral

Paper↗

01 — The idea

An AI agent's personality is just a prompt away. We randomized it to ask which pairings work.

Each person built real display ads with an AI agent prompted to be high or low on each of the Big Five traits. Because we randomized the agent's personality, the differences across pairings are causal. The same pairing reliably produces the same result.
